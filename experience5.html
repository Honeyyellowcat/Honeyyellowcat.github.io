Flight Dynamics Operations Engineer

Summary

As a Flight Dynamics Operations Engineer, I support satellite operations across all mission phases, assisting with orbital maneuvers and constellation alignment. I collaborate with multiple teams to execute procedures, monitor performance, and respond to anomalies.

Launch & Pre-Launch Operations

  • Prepare satellites for launch: maintain documentation, review parameters, conduct risk assessments, and ensure operational readiness.

    Operational Procedures Mission Coordination Anomaly Monitoring

  • Coordinate launch and early orbit operations, track telemetry, confirm deployment, and respond to anomalies in real-time.

    Cross-Team Coordination Launch & Early Orbit Operations Reporting

Post-Launch & Constellation Operations

  • Conduct post-deployment checks, monitor orbit and systems, support early maneuvers, and confirm operational readiness.

    Early Orbit Ops Orbit Monitoring Team Coordination Anomaly Handling

  • Maintain constellation geometry, perform station-keeping maneuvers, and plan end-of-life disposal procedures.

    Orbit Maintenance Trajectory Planning End-of-Life Ops

Skills & Tools Learned

  • Programming: Supporting operational and data tasks.
  • Team Collaboration: Coordinating with multiple teams.
  • Risk & Decision-Making: Informed operational choices in dynamic scenarios.
  • Operational Oversight: Tracking mission progress and maintaining records.

Professional Growth & Key Achievements

I contributed to multi-satellite operations across all phases, supporting operational continuity and coordinated maneuvers. This experience strengthened my technical expertise, problem-solving abilities, and decision-making skills.

  • Improved prelaunch procedures, reducing errors and enhancing readiness.
  • Supported satellite maneuvers, ensuring smooth constellation operations.
  • Addressed operational anomalies, maintaining mission continuity.
  • Developed workflows for data tasks, streamlining decision-making.
  • Enhanced team communication, improving coordination across functions.

This role allowed me to grow technically and professionally, building resilience, leadership, and a broad understanding of flight dynamics operations.
